As a suggestion for Computer Science students. I sorted out some content and wanted to share with you.



After waking up every morning, breakfast can give you a good spirit of the day. And for the breakfast, I think Coffee Revolution must be the best place to go! Have a locally sourced cakes and sandwiches with a cup of coffee would be best.



In the intense study, it is also a good choice to buy small snacks in Our Shop. Many products are available for you to choose from.



After completing your day of study, you can go to the bar to relax and socialize. And the Bar One is giving us this platform. Bar One is famous for towering tasty burgers, regular quizzes, all the best sport and a whole lot of friendly staff!



Because our university has a variety of facilities, we can get a lot of help in Welcome desk, if you have any questions, you can solve it there.
