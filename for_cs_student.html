As a suggestion for Computer Science students.I sorted out some content and wanted to share with you.

After waking up every morning, breakfast can give you a good spirit of the day. And for the breakfast, I think Coffee Revolution (https://su.sheffield.ac.uk/ coffee-revolution) must be the best place to go! Have a locally sourced cakes and sandwiches with a cup of coffee would be best.

In the intense study, it is also a good choice to buy small snacks in Our Shop （https://su.sheffield.ac.uk/eat-drink-shop）. Many products are available for you to choose from.

After completing your day of study, you can go to the bar to relax and socialize. And the Bar One (https://su.sheffield.ac.uk/bar-one) is giving us this platform. Bar One is famous for towering tasty burgers, regular quizzes, all the best sport and a whole lot of friendly staff!

Because our university has a variety of facilities, we can get a lot of help in Welcome desk (https://su.sheffield.ac.uk/food-drink/outlets-services-pages/welcome-desk), if you have any questions, you can solve it there.
